Services

We've Got You Covered

General & Urgent Care

Primary Care

Our medical services include infant and well child checkups, immunizations, health screenings and women's health.

Learn More →

Dental Care

Full dental care for the whole family includes check-ups, cleanings, restoration and urgent care.

Learn More →

After Hours Clinic

For illness and injuries after 5 pm, come to our Holly Park clinic in south Seattle.

Learn More →

Pharmacy

Services include specialty pharmacy, compliance packaging, vaccines, and immunizations.

Learn More →

Sexual Health & Wellness

Sexual health resources: contraception, PrEP, and STI testing, treatment, and prevention.

Learn More →

Eye Care

Complete eye exam services for adults and children.

Learn More →
Specialized Care

Pregnancy Care

Care through pregnancy, during childbirth, and following birth for you and your baby.

Learn More →

Geriatric Care

High-quality, patient-centered care for older adults.

Learn More →

Behavioral Health

Professional consultation and treatment for depression, anxiety, relationship, and lifestyle issues.

Learn More →

Medication Assisted Treatment

Compassionate care of prescription pain medication and heroin addiction.

Learn More →

School Services

On-site primary care, behavioral health services and restorative/preventive dental care for students.

Learn More →

Integrative Medicine

Whole person health care that combines conventional medicine with complementary therapies.

Learn More →
Wellness & Community Support

Women, Infants, & Children (WIC)

Women, Infants, & Children (WIC) nutrition program for qualified families.

Learn More →

Healthy Aging & Wellness

Multi culturally appropriate elder care for the Asian community.

Learn More →

Nutrition Services

Health education and management with nutrition-based treatment from registered dietitians and nutritionists.

Learn More →

Identity-Support

Comprehensive healthcare: primary, hormonal, sexual, and behavioral health.

Learn More →

Acupuncture

Our certified practitioners focus on chronic pain, reduction of polypharmacy, cancer side effect treatments, and more.

Learn More →

Health Education

Our community health specialists will help you access the care you need.

Learn More →

Here For You

Fully access our affordable, quality health care services. All are welcome at ICHS, regardless of insurance or ability to pay.

What We Offer

Our Services Include

Comprehensive care for your whole family, from newborns to seniors — all in a welcoming, culturally responsive environment.

Preventive Care

Stay healthy with routine check-ups, vaccinations, screenings, and personalized wellness plans designed to prevent illness before it starts.

Schedule a check-up →
After Hours Care

Extended evening and weekend hours so care fits your schedule, not the other way around.

Schedule a check-up →
Child Checkups

Well-child visits, developmental screenings, and immunizations to keep your children healthy and thriving at every age.

Book for your child →
Women's Health

Comprehensive gynecological care, prenatal services, and reproductive health support tailored to your needs and values.

Learn More →
Family Planning

Contraception counseling, fertility awareness, and family planning resources tailored to your needs and values.

Learn More →
Geriatrics

Specialized medical and wellness support for older adults.

Learn More →
